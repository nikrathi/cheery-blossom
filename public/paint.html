2:30 PM

Cherry blossom painting

Settle in after lunch and paint soft branches, petals, and spring skies in your own style.

  • Theme: cherry blossom inspired
  • Focus: soft pinks, florals, branches, lantern light, spring mood
  • Format: individual canvas or sheet
  • Pace: relaxed and social
4:30 PM

Pass the Paint

One canvas is colored by multiple people. Each person adds a small piece, then passes it to the next person.

  • One shared canvas for the whole group
  • Each guest paints for a short turn
  • Canvas rotates around the table
  • The final piece becomes a full group artwork
